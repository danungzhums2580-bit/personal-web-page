MY FAVOURITE MOVIES

  • The day of the jakal

    • The narrative showcases the cold professionalism of an anonymous assassin,
    known only as the "Jackal," as he plots to kill French President Charles de Gaulle,
    while a dogged and methodical detective races against time to stop him.

  • Game of Thrones

    • A complex fantasy drama about a brutal, multi-generational struggle for the Iron Throne
    in the fictional land of Westeros. The core conflict involves several powerful noble families
    vying for control, a fight complicated by shifting alliances, betrayals, and a looming, ancient
    threat of ice zombies called White Walkers descending from the North. It is known for its dark, realistic
    take on medieval-style politics, focusing on political intrigue, power, and the human cost of war, rather
    than simply magic.

  • Queen of the South

    • Action-packed crime drama that chronicles the remarkable and often brutal transformation of Teresa Mendoza
    from a poor, innocent woman into a cunning, powerful drug cartel leader. It's a gripping story of survival,
    ambition, and the emotional cost of power.
